  • This is our final project! It is a simple game that plays by pressing the space button. It is a parody of the extremely popular game Flappy Bird however it uses the bitmojis we created as part of our class assignments instead of the Bird. By pressing down on the spacebar the bitmoji moves up and once you let go it goes down. The point of the game is to avoid the green pipes and if you collide with the pipes then you die and are presented with an instant game over screen.
